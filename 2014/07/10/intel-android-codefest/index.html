INTEL Android Codefest

Created at 2014-07-10 Updated at 2025-09-05 - 1 min. read Category work Tag android / INTEL / XAHM / XDK

When i received the confirmation call from INTEL. I was very excited.Venue was The Park Hotel. i arrived on time. Registration process was quite fast and soon i got my ID.  The talk began with introduction to all INTEL technology of game development with android. Till lunch time it was all introduction part going on with contest of selfie.

After lunch there were quite interesting session from building your native app to how to configure and run your native app. Demonstration of the INTEL XDK to the emulatorXAHM(fast than native one).  The best part was the debugging session where you can debug each and every function to the each and every frame. The debugging tool gave visual graph of each CORE use or battery utilization to many more option.

Looking forward for next INTEL event.
