Data Meet @ Google

Created at 2014-03-21 Updated at 2025-09-05 - 1 min. read Category experience Tag android / application / google / know ur representative

Day started quite well.We reached Google early in the morning. 

The talk started on election data as theme was election. I and Varun Kakkar was there to showcase our android application Know Your Representative. Unfortunately due to less time span our talk was rescheduled on next day.  Next day session started with our talk on our application.It was also a great privilege to have our mentor Satyaakam Goswami with us. It was my first talk on this level of platform. It went smooth with many feedbacks and people were excited to know that how we used open government data from Data.gov to a useful application. After the session we took part in hackathon which was worth as it’s just a beginning for me.
