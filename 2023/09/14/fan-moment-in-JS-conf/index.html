fan moment in JS conf

Created at 2023-09-14 Updated at 2025-09-05 - 2 min. read

Me and my team(aryan, karthik) was lucky enough to go to js conf 2023. Our primarly aim was to explore the new talks and understand about the new things. Js conf was full of fun. As there was different section where all the sponsors were explaining about the company as well as there were js quizes and problem to solve. We majorly attended talks as there were two tracks. At times it was way difficult to choose as which one to attend. But all the sessions were going to record so it was clear that we can see them afterwards as well.

What we were waiting for was the last talk which was of Kyle Simpson. He talked about the future of the web. Where being offline or the area where there is not feasible internet service. In those areas how should an application works. He talks about how would future be looking to this issue. As framework and libraries would be coming and going but the real issue remains of the internet. It was fabulous talk enjoyed every bit of it.

As soon as the talk was over we ran at the back stage just to meet him as we were already getting late for the flight. As I have been grown up in js by just watching his videos and reading his books. So as soon as I met. I was blank. It was way a BIG fan moment. We got some pics tried to tell him how much he was important for us. At the end he was kind enough to say. Go and catch you flight or else it will be missed.
