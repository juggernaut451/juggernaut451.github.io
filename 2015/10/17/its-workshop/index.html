ITS Workshop

Created at 2015-10-18 Updated at 2025-09-05 - 1 min. read Category experience / work Tag greater noida / pydelhi / python.its / volunteer / workshop

It was the first workshop that was closest to my home i.e ITS, Greater Noida. The talk was given by Anuvrat. It was about Basics of Python. I with my friends(Nupur and Rishabh) were volunteering for this event. Crowd consisted of final year student of computer science and Information technology branch. In the talk we also discussed about the Pydelhi. That how the community can help the growth of the individual as well as how you as a individual can contribute to the community. Now, eagerly waiting for the next event with Pydelhi.
