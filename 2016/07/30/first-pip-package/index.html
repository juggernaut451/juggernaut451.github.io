While doing my internship in RoadToNaukri.com. We came to a problem statement where we had to find whether a moblie number is in DND or not. Those who don’t know DND means Do Not Disturb. This is an initiative by TRAI (Telecom Regulatory Authority of India) to save consumers from unsolicited calls/SMS. If a person registers his/her mobile number, then it would be illegal for telemarketers to send any sort of unsolicited SMS or calls on those numbers.
